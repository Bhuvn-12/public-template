We believe finance should not just be a "RUN" the business function anymore that is concerned only with collecting and reporting of financial results. Rather it should also be an enabler for "CHANGE" in the business by providing strategic business insights for management decision making. We have built Finance Period End Management (FPEM) solution with following features and benefits to enable this transformation. Organize your finance processes around a single source of truth enabled by FPEM that contains reconciled/fully attributed customer-product detail and transform Finance from a labor-intensive compilation activity into a value-added function that creates transparent outputs useful for internal and external consumption and extension.

Timely

FPEM is built from ground up to provide you with the latest and upto the moment financial information that is in sync with your sources.

Straight through processing

FPEM integrates with product sources in "Straight through processing" framework allowing processing of source system business events in real time, shifting energy invested in finance from “getting the numbers out” to “making the numbers right”. Straight through processing will automate assembly of numbers so finance work will shift from the low-value mechanics of collecting, assembling and reconciling numbers to scrubbing the numbers for errors/omissions and analyzing/reporting the numbers in ways that illuminate and characterize the multi-dimensional aspects of the business in ways that “point the way forward”.

Daily Close

Do you feel like you are under the gun every financial period end to close the books on time? Do you want to take down the time it takes to close your books? We have built FPEM solution from ground up to account for features like - (a) Predict / Forecast missing source system information to fill in the blanks while waiting for source systems to send all the information and (b) Do soft close every day so that you can see you complete trial balance (balance sheet and income statement) as it would/should be like had all the information was available right now.

Benefits

"- Period end is like any other day. - Reduce errors and ommisions through straight through integration and processing of source system data. - Correct errors and ommisions by working on these during the whole month as opposed to just period end. - Monitor critical items ( e.g. liquidity ratios ) real time for timely decision making."

Transparent

FPEM is built from ground up to provide not only the flexibility and control of your accounting process but also ability to manage this at lowest grain of business activities driving the business results.

Rules Based Accounting

FPEM comes with a business user friendly "Accounting Rules Engine" that allows business to maintain control of the accounting rules and analyze results in a transparent fashion. You can drive ownership and accountability for maintaing global accounting mandates with local reagional nuances through accounting rules. And business ownership in driving creation, modifications and application of these rules in FPEM environment not only increases compliance transparency but also reduces your dependence on IT and others to effect change.

Instrument Ledger

Obviously, you do have a choice of many accounting softwares. But not many (in-fact none, at least at high volumes) perform accoutning at the lowest instrument level. We have built FPEM from ground up to perform accounting at instrument level for each business event. That allows us to provide you aggregated ledger views and seamless drill down to lower product, customer and instrument level sub-ledger views in automatic, reconciled and available for all to explore transparent fashion. Imagine this -> you providing a user-id and password to your auditor as opposed to a team of accountants to answer his/her queries.

Benefits

"- Increase control through ownership of accounting rules. - Provide greater flexibility for product owners as well as global and local accountants to cooperate in owning and maintaining accounting rules. - Increase transparency through automatically reconciled ledger and sub-ledgers at instrument levels."
